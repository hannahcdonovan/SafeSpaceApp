Bowdoin College Safe Space

After launching our support line in the Spring of 2018, Safe Space made it their club's mission is to make resources more available, and accessible to survivors of sexual assault on our campus. With the launch of this website, we hope survivors find another safe space on this campus right here.

On this site, whether you are a survivor, friend, or anyone with curiousity for the cause, you will find an online chatroom that will connect you to other Bowdoin advocates on campus. Furthermore, this site offers a place for individuals to connect to the Bowdoin Title IX website.
